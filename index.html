Aloe plants grow in many colors and shapes, including variegated, spiny and/or thick leaves. The leaves are thick to help store water during drought periods, so they thrive as houseplants or in arid climates. Aloe vera is the most common species of aloe, and can be found in nearly any garden store.

Ordinary Aloe can become a tropical centerpiece with the right pot.

With PrintAPot's Golden Pineapple Pot, you can make your aloe vera truly stand out. The Golden Pineapple Pot has an awesome tropical, warm aesthetic. The golden color is reminiscent of the softest, sweetest pineapple flesh you've ever tasted. Since aloe plants enjoy arid conditions and dry soil, there are also two large drainage holes on the bottom for keeping your aloe plants happy and healthy.

Modern Floating Planter

Orchids are a widespread family of flowering plants, well known for the unique beauty of their blooms. Their flowers are typically aromatic and occur along a stem, or inflorescence. Their leaves split from the base of the plant to form a symmetric ladder of leaves rising to support the stems.

The intersecting geometric shapes that create the Modern Floating Planter are naturally complementary to orchids, and a host of other plant arrangements, including this succulent arrangement. Curving, flowing ribbons support the lofted square planter as it floats above the circular drainage tray.

Not even a hurricane could overwater this planter.

The drainage tray is removable with a twisting lock feature. These features allow excess water to be collected and reused to water other plants. This pot is so effective at water drainage that the 2017 Hurricane Irma rainstorms drained right out and the plants flourished despite being effectively overwatered for 5 days in a row.
